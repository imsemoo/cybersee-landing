Exposure Types

What We Flag
as Risk

  • Unknown or forgotten subdomains
  • Exposed admin / login panels
  • Development environments accessible externally
  • Expired or weak TLS certificates
  • Services with known CVEs
  • Misconfigured cloud storage or metadata
  • Default credentials or exposed config files
  • Legacy technology stacks past EOL
