Early Detection of
Info-Stealer Infections

Info-stealers exfiltrate credentials, session cookies, and browser data from infected devices. Cybersee surfaces these logs so your team can contain infections and reset access before attackers act.

The Threat

Info-Stealers Are
a Primary Attack Vector

Modern ransomware and fraud operations routinely start with stealer log purchases. A single infected employee device can expose credentials for dozens of corporate systems — often with active session cookies that bypass MFA.
