SLA & Expectations

Realistic Timelines,
Structured Process

Takedown timelines vary depending on the platform, registrar, hosting provider, and evidence requirements. Cybersee helps structure and accelerate the process through documented workflows, evidence packages, and partner-supported escalation where applicable.

  1. Phishing Domain Detected
    T + 00:00

    Lookalike domain flagged via typosquatting monitor. Evidence captured automatically.

  2. Evidence Package Compiled
    T + 12 min

    WHOIS, hosting provider, screenshots, and brand-ownership proof bundled into registrar-ready submission.

  3. Takedown Request Filed
    T + 45 min

    Submitted to registrar & hosting provider abuse channels. Case tracked with unique ID.

  4. Escalated to Partners
    T + 6 hr

    No response from registrar — escalated through industry takedown partners and browser blocklists.

  5. Domain Suspended
    T + 18 hr

    Domain taken offline. Case closed with full audit trail delivered to the requesting team.
